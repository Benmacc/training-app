Data Backup
Export your data regularly and save it to Notes or Messages as a backup. If your data is ever lost, paste it back in to restore everything instantly.
How to back up
1. Tap Export Data above
2. Open your Notes app
3. Create a note called "BYU Trainer Backup"
4. Paste and save
5. Repeat weekly to stay protected
How to restore
1. Open your Notes backup
2. Select all and copy the text
3. Tap Import Data above
4. Paste and tap Restore
5. All your data will be back instantly
